Bombhunter


Description:


Bomb Hunter is a simple game created in Unity, whereby the gamescope is that:
- The Bomb monsters will spawn at random
- Using the sword provided, the players (bomb hunters) must hit the bomb monsters
- Upon hitting them, it will explode and disappear
- Clicking on the bomb monsters will make them dizzy
