Alphabets Typography


Description:


For my namecard, I selected a blueish color that fades when it goes upwards (gradient), while the back of the namecard is white. I feel that it is a great combination as the blue is not too striking, making it easy to see. I chose a white logo on dark blue background as it gives a contrast, as I don't want it to be too striking too.

On the back of the namecard, I have also added the same background shapes and texture, but made it white with a lower opacity so that the text is readable. The background also have some designs, so as to give some shapes and texture to the namecard, which is also flushed alongside my name and designation. As the back of the namecard is white, I've decided to go with black text as it is basic and readable, and not flashy at all. I have also added a line in-between my name and the icons/socials to better separate the sections and give some space between them too.

Typography Used:
- Source Sans Variable
