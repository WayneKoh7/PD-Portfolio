SOI Learning Portal


Description:


The School of Infocomm (SOI) Learning Portal is an online directory of coding courses that makes it simple for you to enrol in the courses that most closely match your interests and learning requirements.
We were tasked to code the home page of the SOI Learning Portal using bootstrap, as well as the front-end needs which will be integrated with the back-end for retrieval of data from the database.

The home page which I have coded includes a teacher, who's background I have removed from using Adobe Photoshop. This allows the photo to be included into the website without any background that disturbs the website. The website also includes a drop-down menu for students to choose from, and a search bar. It also includes notifications and profile, which can be turned on or off to switch between online and offline mode. Below, I have added a three column section, as well as a carousel of courses for students to choose from. Students can also bookmark the courses which will be saved and a offcanvas body will appear at the bottom of the page.
